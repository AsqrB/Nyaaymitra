What are we best at

Our services

Arbitration

Arbitration resolves disputes by having an impartial third party (the arbitrator) make a final decision. It’s like a private court process where the arbitrator’s decision is binding.

Negotiation

Negotiation helps people or parties communicate directly to settle a disagreement or reach an agreement. Both sides discuss and make compromises to find a solution that works for everyone.

Mediation

Mediation uses a neutral third party (the mediator) to help people in conflict reach an agreement. The mediator doesn’t decide the outcome but facilitates communication to help both parties find common ground.

Criminal Law

Criminal law aims to maintain justice, deter crime, and protect society by defining and punishing unlawful acts.

Civil Law

Civil law resolves disputes between individuals or entities, focusing on compensation and rights rather than punishment for wrongdoing.

Consulting

Consulting provides expert advice and solutions to organizations or individuals to improve performance, solve problems, or achieve specific goals.

Process Flow

Registration

Register your case and propose a settlement offer

Intimation

Offer is transmitted to the other party over email/SMS/WhatsApp/post with option to accept/propose counter-offer

Negotiation

AI-driven negotiation is facilitated based on offer and proposed by the parties

Settlement

Upon successful negotiations, the agreed terms are documented in a settlement agreement.
